Hyena

Hyena are one of the best mothers in the animal kingdom, investing more energy per cub than any other terrestial carnivore.

  • kingdom: Animalia
  • phylum: Chordata
  • Class: Mammalia
  • Order: Carnivora
  • Family: Hyaenide

Hyenas are more than the carrion eating villians of the lion King. Though you may not ever fall in love with these cunning animals, its hard not to respect them.
